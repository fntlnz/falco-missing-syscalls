Falco missing syscalls
by @fntlnz

What is this?

the kernel is a constantly evolving project and syscalls are added at every release. Falco is heavily based on syscalls and if you want to do a contribution, adding support for a syscall is always a good thing to do!
Syscall fillers are a crucial part to implement to avoid Falco bypasses as much as possible. This page is inspired by the talk "Bypass Falco" that @leodido gave at Kubecon Virtual NA, specifically the support missing syscalls part of it.
